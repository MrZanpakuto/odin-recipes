Franks & Beans

Description

Classic Franks and Bean Recipe with a kick!

Ingredients

  • Nathan Hotdogs
  • Baked Beans
  • Franks Hot Sauce

Instructions

  1. Slice up hot dogs into little pieces
  2. Sear hot dogs in pan with a small amount of water or oil
  3. Combined cooked hot dogs with beans and simmer until warm
  4. Add Franks Hot Sauce for a special kick
