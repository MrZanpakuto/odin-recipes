PB&J Delight

Description

Classic Peanut Butter & Jelly sandwhich with a little bit of crisp!

Ingredients

  • Bread of Choice
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly or Jam of Choice
  • Bananas (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast Bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on toast. Repeat on other piece of toast with Jelly.
  3. Lay peanut butter toast on top of jelly toast
