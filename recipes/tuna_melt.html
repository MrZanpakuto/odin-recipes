Tuna Melt Sandwhich

Description

A cheesy sandwich filled with tuna and mayo always seems to hit the spot.

Ingredients

  • Bread of Choice
  • Tuna
  • Mayo
  • Relish

Instructions

  1. Combine Mayo, Relish, & Tuna. Mix well
  2. Toast Bread
  3. Spread mixture on toast and make sandwich
